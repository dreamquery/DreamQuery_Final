Batch Enable / Disable

Overview
This feature enables you to batch enable various options for quickness.
Categories
Specify which categories this update applies to. Categories and any products in selected categories are affected.
Apply Following Action
Please choose actions from the following:

Enable Products - Enables products in selected categories.
Disable Products - Disables products in selected categories.

Enable Categories - Enables selected categories.
Disable Categories - Disables selected categories.

Enable Brands - Enables brands for products in selected categories.
Disable Brands - Disables brands for products in selected categories.

Enable Disqus - Enables disqus comments for products in selected categories and also categories selected.
Disable Disqus - Disables disqus comments for products in selected categories and also categories selected.

Free Shipping Off - If applicable, disables the free shipping option for products in selected categories and also categories selected.
Free Shipping On - If applicable, enables the free shipping option for products in selected categories and also categories selected.

Cart Purchase On - Enables cart purchase for products in selected categories.
Cart Purchase Off - Disables cart purchase for products in selected categories.
