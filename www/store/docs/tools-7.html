Search Log

Overview
This feature lets you view search keywords entered in your store searches. Useful to see what people are searching for if you need to improve your inventory.

Search log is disabled/enabled in settings.

It is recommended you export the logs from time to time and/or clear the log table as this could build up and slow down your database.

Note: What you see here may have nothing to do with your store. Some people get a kick out of searching for stupid things. :)
Top Bar Options
- Click to filter search results by searches that generated no results.

- Save search to CSV

- Clear search log
