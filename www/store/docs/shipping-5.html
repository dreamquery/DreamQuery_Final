Batch Update Rates

Overview
Here you can batch update your shipping rates. Adjustments can be applied to all rates or only rates in selected zones. Rate adjustment can be a fixed amount or a percentage. This is useful if you need to quickly batch update your rates due to price increases.
Shipping Rate Adjustment
Specify rate adjustment. This can be a set amount or a percentage for increases or decreases OR a fixed amount. Examples:

9.99 or 10% (For Increase or Decrease)
9.99 (Fixed)

No currency symbol for price and NO commas.

Note that for percentage based adjustments, amount entered is ALWAYS a percent. For example, if your percentage based rates are 10% and you enter 5.55, this would mean 5.55% and not 5.55 as a price.
Adjustment Type
Please choose:

Increase or Decrease - Takes existing price/rate and increases or decreases price/rate by amount specified.

Fixed - A new fixed price/rate.
Apply to Shipping Rates in Selected Zones
Specify which zones to apply this adjustment to. Use the checkboxes to make your selections.
Update Rate Preference
Specify which rates to adjust:

Flat Rates - Flat rates for selected zone(s)
Per Item Rates - Per item rates for selected zone(s)
Percentage Rates - Percentage based rates for selected zone(s)
Weight Based Rates - Weight based rates for selected zone(s)
Tare Weight Rates - Tare weight based rates

At least 1 option MUST be specified. If none are specified, defaults to weight based ONLY.
Additional Item Adjustment
For Per Item rates only. This is the adjustment for the additional items price. Same rules apply as for the shipping rate adjustment. Leave blank to NOT adjust the additional items price.
