In Stock Overview

Overview
This page lets you see a stock overview of your products and attributes. Current stock is shown in brackets.

You can filter by category via the icon, export the results to csv via the icon and batch update stock via the checkboxes.

if category filter is in used, export will follow filter.

To update stock level of individual product, click the product name/attribute to edit.
Export Prefixes
When you export, products are prefixed (P) and attributes (A). To change this edit:

define('IN_STOCK_PREFIX_PRODUCTS', '(P) ');
define('IN_STOCK_PREFIX_ATTRIBUTES', '(A) ');

In the following file:

admin/control/defined.php
