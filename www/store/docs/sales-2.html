Sales Overview

Overview
Enables you see which products are the best sellers in your store and what profit you have made against your cost prices.

Attribute calculations are not taken into account on this screen.
Filters
Click the icon to filter by category and/or date range.
Export
Overview can be exported to CSV if required via the icon.
Profit
Calculated on the cost price. If you have sold items less than the cost price, this will impact profits. Click the profit amount to launch calculation window.
