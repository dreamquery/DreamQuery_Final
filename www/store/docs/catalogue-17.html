Gift Certificates

Overview
Gift certificates are purchasable items in your store. A buyer can specify a recipient for the certificate, who can then use the certificate in your store. Gift certificates are a popular way for people to exchange gift ideas. The recipient can choose to spend the money on whatever he/she chooses in your store.

Codes are automatically generated and emailed to recipient when a certificate is purchased. The recipient can choose to spend the whole gift certificate or part of the certificate and redeem the balance later. There is no expiry on gift certificates.

Gift certificates should not be confused with discount coupons, which are admin created discounts.
Certificate Name
Enter name for gift certificate. Max 250 characters.
Display Image
Upload display image. Usually a gift certificate mock up. This is optional. Recommended size 150 x 60 pixels.

There are a lot of graphic programs or word processors (some free) where you can easily create gift certificate mock ups. You can also purchase gift certificate background stock images from sites such as Dreamstime.

Examples from Google Search.
Value
Enter value for gift certificate. eg: 9.99. No commas or currency symbol.
Enabled
Enable or disable gift certificate. Disabling a gift certificate does not disable previously purchased certificates.
Edit/Delete
Use the options to edit or delete gift certificates.
Gift Certificate Ordering
To change the order the gift certificates appear, hover your cursor over the certificate rows until you see a crosshair. Click and drag to re-order.
Gift Report
Click the icon to view an overview of gift certificate usage. From this screen you can view the intial purchase sale, or search for sales where a gift certificate has been redeemed.
