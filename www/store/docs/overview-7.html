Maian Cart Overview - RSS Feeds

RSS Feeds
RSS Feeds enable visitors to subscribe to categories, special offers and latest additions etc anonymously. A feed reader is required to view RSS feeds and there are many free readers about including RSS Owl, QuiteRSS as well as cloud services such as inoReader & Feedly. More info on RSS feeds here.

Feeds are available throughout the Maian Cart system. The latest products feed is the general feed, this can be accessed via the icon in the footer.

Are available for special offers, categories etc. Look for the icon.
