Shipping Rates

Overview
At the moment the following shipping routines are supported by Maian Cart:

Flat Rates - Flat rate shipping charge no matter how many items are in the cart.
Per Item Rates - Allow you to set the shipping cost for the first item and set a charge per item after that.
Percentage Rates - Based on the goods total of tangible items.
Quantity Rates - Based on the quantity of tangible items.
Weight Rates - Based on the weight of tangible items and grouped into weight bands.

The hierarchy of these can be changed by dragging and dropping the rows. See the following screenshot:


Note that the order isn`t completed until the 'Update' button is clicked.

The system will check the availability of shipping rates in the order set. If it finds a match it returns the shipping cost.

You can have different options for different countries if you wish. For example, if you are UK based, you may prefer a flat rate for the UK, but weight based for the U.S.
Enable / Disable
If you want to enable or disable a shipping option, use the checkbox on the relevant row, then click the 'Update' button.
