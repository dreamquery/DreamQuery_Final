Manage Products

Overview
Here you can esily manage products. Please read below for more information.
Filters / Search
Are available via the and icons.
Stock Level Change
Stock level can be quickly updated by clicking on the stock amount.

Visible Icons
The following are available for all products:

- View product in store

- Edit product

- Copy product

- Product notes

- Click to view product image (if applicable). This is for small screens ONLY. On larger screens, image preview is shown by default
Additional Options
Are accessed by clicking the icon:

- Product Pictures

- Related Products

- MP3 Previews

- Personalisation

- Attributes

- Copy Attributes

- Buy Now Button (Ideal for forums. Same as adding item to basket and loading checkout screen, only from a direct link)

- Delete Product
Enable / Disable
Click the 'Enabled / Disabled' text to quickly disable or enable product.
Batch Edit
Use the checkboxes provided and click the button at the bottom of the page to batch edit. More info here.

Products can also be batch updated from CSV.
Batch Delete
Use the checkboxes provided and click the button at the bottom of the page to batch delete.
