Edit Sale

Overview
Here you can edit an existing sale. This is the same as the add sale screen, with a few small differences as shown below.
Remove Product from Sale
To remove a product from a sale, simply set the quantity to 0. The product will highlight and be removed on update.


If you remove all products from a sale and click update, the sale is deleted. If you want to replace a product and the sale only has one product, add the new product first before deleting the old one.
Add Products to Sale
To add products to sale, click the navigational button.

Account Tab
This isn`t needed on the sale edit screen.
