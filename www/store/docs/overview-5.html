Maian Cart Overview - Gift Certificates

Gift Certificates
Buyers can purchase gift certificates and have them sent to a friend so they themselves can purchase from the store.
Gift Certificate Link
If there is at least 1 enabled gift certificate, the gift certificate link will appear as a category.


By default the gift certificate link will appear at the bottom of your menu, but it is possible to position the gift certificate link after or before an existing category via the settings.
Display
The gift certificate screen is fairly simple. Buyer chooses gift certificate, enters from/to information and adds to cart. Message can also be included, but is optional. If included is added to email sent to recipient with gift code.

Recipient
Once payment is completed, recipient is emailed their gift code for use in the store.
