Price Points

Overview
Price points are a useful addition to your store. They enable people to view all products between a specified price range or price point. For example, you may have lots of products and someone may just be looking for a gift between a given range based on their budget. This option appears in the left hand menu and is entirely optional. See the demo to see how it works.
Adding Price Points
Enter a 'Price From' and a 'Price To' amount. If you wish you can enter alternative text to appear instead of the price point range. For example, if you enter '0.00' as the price from amount and '9.99' as the price to amount, without alternative text you would see the range as the link instead of the text. Again, see the demo or tutorial videos if available.

NOTE You should always make sure that each price point is not in the same range as a previous price point. For example if you specified:

0.00 - 5.00
5.00 - 10.00

and someone did a search for items that were 5.00 - 10.00, this would fall into both criteria as the first range is also up to 5.00. Instead you should do:

0.00 - 5.00
5.01 - 10.00
10.01 - 20.00

etc
Drag and Drop to Order
To set the order of the display price point links, drag and drop the rows after creating price points. A move icon appears if you hover your cursor over the rows. Simply drag and drop to re-order.
