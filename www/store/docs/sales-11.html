Sales Revenue

Overview
This feature lets you see your sales revenue between selected dates and export to CSV. Useful for tax purposes.
Filters
Revenue can be filtered by payment statuses or date range via the icon. The default view is the current week for 'Completed' orders. Use the options provided to adjust filter.
Export Revenue
Exports revenue view to CSV via the icon. If you have used the filters, this will also be replicated to the CSV.
