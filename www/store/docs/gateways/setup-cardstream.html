CardStream - Setup
1: Sign up for a Cardstream account to receive your merchant information.
2: Enter applicable details in your Maian Cart admin area:

Sales > Payment Methods > Cardstream
Cardstream - Callback Url
The callback url for all operations is:

http://www.example.com/checkout/cardstream.php
https://www.example.com/checkout/cardstream.php (SSL)

Where 'example.com' is your own domain.
