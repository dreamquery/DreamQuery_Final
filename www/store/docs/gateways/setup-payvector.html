Pay Vector - Setup
1: Sign up for a Pay Vector account to receive your merchant id and password.
2: Log into your Pay Vector account.
3: Make sure the hash method for your pre-share key is set to SHA1.
4: Enter applicable details in your Maian Cart admin area:

Sales > Payment Methods > Pay Vector
Pay Vector - Callback Url
The callback url for all operations is:

http://www.example.com/checkout/payvector.php
https://www.example.com/checkout/payvector.php (SSL)

Where 'example.com' is your own domain.
