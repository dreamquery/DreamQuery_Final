CardSave - Setup
1: Sign up for a CardSave account to receive your merchant id and password.
2: Log into your CardSave account.
3: Make sure the hash method for your pre-share key is set to SHA1.
4: Enter applicable details in your Maian Cart admin area:

Sales > Payment Methods > CardSave
Cardsave - Callback Url
The callback url for all operations is:

http://www.example.com/checkout/cardsave.php
https://www.example.com/checkout/cardsave.php (SSL)

Where 'example.com' is your own domain.
