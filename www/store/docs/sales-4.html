Add Tangible / Downloadable Goods to Sale

Overview
Here you can easily add products to an existing or new sale.
Categories
Choose a category to view products in that category.
Add
Use the checkboxes to select the products you want to add. Products in existing sales won`t be shown. Once you have selected a product the sale screen will reload. Follow same procedure to add other products.
Cancel
Click to cancel operation.
Quick Links
For existing sales, the quick links are shown for additional navigation.
