Products Ordered

Overview
This screen offers a quick glance of the products in the sale. From this screen you can resend emails to drop shippers if applicable.
Product Qty Bar
Clicking the icon may show one or more of the following:

- Edit Product - If shown, click to load product edit screen (tangible or gift).

- Gift Certificate Management - If shown, click to load gift certificate management screen.

- Download Management - If shown, click to load download management screen.

- XXX (Drop Shipping Supplier). If shown, click to load drop shipper edit screen.
Resend Email to Drop Shippers
Only available if at least 1 product is applicable for a drop ship supplier. Check the boxes shown and then click the "Resend Shipping Confirmation to Drop Shipping Supplier(s)" button at the bottom of the page. Email will be resent the same as when an order is completed. This option is useful if an email wasn`t sent when the order completed OR you prefer to send manually.

APIs
If you are utilising Maian Cube and / or Maian Guardian, an APIs option will also be shown. This enables you to send relevant product sales items manually to your cube and/or guardian installations. If checkboxes are present due to drop shippers, you don`t need to check the boxes to send to the APIs. Simply, select the API, confirm and then send. Only relevant products will be sent.
