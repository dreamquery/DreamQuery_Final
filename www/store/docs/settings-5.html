Homepage Product Options

Overview
This feature enables you to display certain products on your homepage. For example, you may have certain products that are on offer that you wish to highlight by showing on your homepage, so they stand out. You may display random or latest products from any number of categories, or display certain products only.
Random or Latest Product Display
Enter an amount of products, random or latest and select categories to display.
Specific Products ONLY:
Certain products can be selected by displaying product ID numbers separated with a comma. Example:

12,98,123,567

To get the product ID, look at your url. Examples:

index.php?pd=45 (Product ID is 45)
product/my-product/65/index.html (Product ID is 65)

Alternatively, to display product IDs, click the icon to show a drop down of categories. Select a category to view a product list with ID numbers.
