Maian Cart Overview - PopUp Basket

PopUp Basket
The pop up basket appears when a product is successfully added to your basket. This is a quick, at a glance basket visible on most pages and accessed via the 'Basket' link on the main menu.


From this basket you can continue to checkout and pay via the link provided.

Removing Items
