Product - Related Products

Overview
Related products are exactly that, related. Related products appear on the product page of the product that it is related to.
Adding New Relation
Select a category to load products for that category. Select applicable products. Use the mirror option if required. If you mirror product relations, this would update across all selected products. For example if you mirror relations and product 4 related to products 1,2 & 3, products 1,2 & 3 would be updated to relate to 4.
Manage Relations
Use the icon to remove product relations.
