Buy Options
A number of buy options (attributes) may also appear beneath the product. Some of these may be required fields and will be shown as such via (R). These are displayed in drop down menus. If applicable, additional cost may also be shown next to each attribute:

Personalisation Options
If enabled for a product, personalisation options may also appear on a product page. Some of these may be required fields and will be shown as such via (R). If applicable, additional cost may also be shown next to each attribute


If options have character limit, the limit will be shown to the right of the box. It will decrement when you start typing:

Add to Cart
To add product to basket, enter quantity and click 'Add to Cart'. If restrictions are set, such as min/max purchase quantity or attributes and/or personalisation isn`t set, an error will appear. Attribute and personalisation boxes will be highlighted if required.

Customers Also Bought / Related Products
If enabled and products exist, these tabs will be shown beneath the product description area / tabs.
