Shipping - Flat Rates

Overview
Flat rate shipping is a single rate offered, not dependant on any other calculations. Whether there are 2 items or 20 items in the basket, the rate remains the same. Flat rates for countries will be applicable for all zones/areas in that country.
Select Zones
Select the zones new rate applies to.
Enter Flat Shipping Rate
Enter new flat rate. No commas or period symbols. eg: 9.99
Enable Flat Rate
Enable or disable this flat rate
Batch Enable/Disable
Click the icon to batch enable or disable rates. Click the link and select the zones applicable.
Manage Rates
Use the links provided to edit or delete rates. Use the icon to filter by enabled countries.
