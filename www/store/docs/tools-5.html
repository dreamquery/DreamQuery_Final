Statistics

Overview
Here you can see a brief overview of how many sales you have processed between given dates and also see which payment methods are most popular if you are using different ones and which countries your buyers come from.

Sales can be filtered by date range via the . This page also shows order statuses.
Sales by Statuses
The first block details sales by statuses. The graph is divided into tangible goods, downloads and gift certificates if applicable. Counts are clickable to load sales screen.
Sales by Payment Method
The pie chart details the most popular payment methods. Methods are clickable to load sales screen.
Sales by Country
The pie chart details the most popular countries. Countries are clickable to load sales screen.
