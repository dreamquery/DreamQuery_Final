Maian Cart Overview - Recently Viewed

Recently Viewed
This provides visitors with an easy at a glance option of which products they have recently viewed.


Recently viewed items are only visible for the duration of the browser session if a visitor isn`t logged in and will auto clear when the browser closes. If a visitor is logged in the recently viewed items are remembered.
Clear Options
Clicking the icon on the header clears recently viewed items. This is session based and may auto clear when the browser is closed as mentioned above.
